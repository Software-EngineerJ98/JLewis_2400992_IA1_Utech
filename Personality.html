My Top 3 Personality Traits

  • Attention to Detail – Along with my tenacity comes my attention to detail.   • Collaboration and Communication – My strong collaboration and communication complete my top 3 traits as they enable me to perform efficiently in a collaborative capacity which is what most organizations including universities require.

Report on AI Business Idea

AI-powered Personalized Study Assistant

This AI app uses natural language processing and machine learning to analyze study habits and generates study plans tailored to individuals.

How It Applies to Me

As a computing student, I juggle many courses. This tool would help optimize my study time by recommending what to review based on my weak areas.

Ethical Issues & Solutions

  • Privacy & Data Security: It uses encryption, anonymization, and asks you if you would like your data to be collected.
  • Bias / Fairness: It learns from various examples and allows customers to adjust it to fit their needs.
  • Over-Reliance: It ensures the app helps users to learn instead of just doing everything for them.
